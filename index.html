UW Data Science Seminar
Showcasing data science in the arts, engineering, humanities and sciences

The UCLA ECE-297 Seminar Series is a department seminar across areas of Electrical and Computer Engineering.

The Fall 2021 seminars focus on the broad areas of Machine Learning, Data Science, and Control and are offered both online and in-person.

Students may opt to sign up for the one credit seminar course ECE-297.

Organization

This seminar is currently organized and hosted by the eScience Institute and the Engineering Data Science Institute (EDSI). We are grateful for the support of the Gordon and Betty Moore Foundationand the Alfred P. Sloan Foundation.

The eScience Institute, iSchool DataLab, andCSE Interactive Data Lab co-created and hosted this seminar series from 2014-2019.
